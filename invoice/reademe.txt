# invoice
Invoice Details:
    invoiceNumber (unique ID).
    dateIssued.
    dueDate.

Seller Details:
    sellerName.
    sellerAddress.
    sellerContact.
    taxRegistrationNumber (optional).

Buyer Details:
    buyerName.
    buyerAddress.
    buyerContact.

Items/Services:
    Array of objects containing:
    itemDescription.
    quantity.
    unitPrice.
    total.

Totals:
    subTotal.
    taxAmount.
    grandTotal.

Payment Details:
    paymentMethod.
    termsAndConditions.
